I got my start writing Java and XML for Android the self-directed way — building real projects, hitting real bugs, and learning the platform from the inside. That foundation is now carrying me through a move to Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, with modern architecture: MVVM, Hilt for DI, and Coroutines/Flow for async work.

I care about apps that are genuinely useful — not just technically sound. My current focus is combining solid Android fundamentals with practical monetization strategy (AdMob) to build apps that are sustainable to run.
